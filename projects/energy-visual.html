exploratory energy

An educational web interactive about energy.

Overview

For something that's supposed to explain even the most fundamental aspects of life, physics is very confusing. Or at least it's often perceived to be, with all its formulas and equations to describe things that are mostly invisible to the naked eye. But memorizing equations doesn't necessarily equate an understanding of the topic - and an understanding doesn't require knowing equations. This webpage incorporates visual elements and interaction to introduce the concept of energy and illustrate important relationships, with the goal of making it less abstract. I made this from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

An intuitive way to visualize energy

Energy in this demo is represented as a dynamic aura that the objects have. The color depicts the type of energy and the size correlates to amount, allowing for a straightforward visualization of the relationship between physical properties (such as height and speed) and energy, as well as a way to actually see how energy transforms from one form to another.

A narrative that shows rather than tells

The demo seeks to engage students without overcomplicating or oversimplifying the concept of energy. The interactive content encourages students to ask questions and see for themselves how manipulating certain elements affects energy rather than being explicitly told how.
